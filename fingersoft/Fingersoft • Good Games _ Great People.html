Fingersoft the company

Fingersoft is one of the most northern game studios in the world, chilling out (literally and figuratively) just 170km south of the Arctic Circle in Oulu, Finland. We are best known for the mobile smash hits Hill Climb Racing and Hill Climb Racing 2, which have a combined total of over 2.5 Billion installs.
